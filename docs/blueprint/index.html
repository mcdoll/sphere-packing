0.1 Introduction

The goal of this project is to formalize the proof that the \(E_8\) lattice minimizes the density of all \(8\)-dimensional sphere packings proved by Maryna Viazovska.

Roughly the proof consists of two steps: the first step is a reduction to finding a function that has certain Fourier theoretic properties, this reduction was proved by Cohn–Elkies in [ . The second step is to explicitly construct a function in terms of weakly modular forms that possess the desired properties. This second step was Viazovska’s contribution and was awarded in 2022 a fields medal.
